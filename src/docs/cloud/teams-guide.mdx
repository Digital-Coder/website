# Teams

Create teams in Umami to organize users and share websites.

## Add a team

Navigate to **Teams** and click on the **Create team** button.

<img src="/images/cloud/team-add.png" />

Fill out the form details and click the **Save** button.

<img src="/images/cloud/team-add-form.png" />

## Share team access code

Navigate to **Teams** and click on the **Edit** button.

<img src="/images/cloud/team-view.png" />

The **Details** tab will show the teams access code. Share this code with users you want to join your team.

<img src="/images/cloud/team-details.png" />

You can generate a new access code by clicking the **Regenerate** button and saving.

## Join a team

Navigate to **Teams** and click on the **Join team** button.

<img src="/images/cloud/team-add.png" />

Enter the **Access code** your received from the team owner and click the **Join** button.

<img src="/images/v2/team-join-form.png" />

## Add a website

Navigate to **Teams** and click on the **View** or **Edit** button.

<img src="/images/cloud/team-view.png" />

Navigate to the **Websites** tab and click on the **Add Website** button.

<img src="/images/cloud/team-website.png" />

Select website(s) and click the **Add Website** button.

<img src="/images/cloud/team-add-website.png" />

All team members can now view the website(s) from the **Websites** tab.

<img src="/images/cloud/team-view-website.png" />

## Remove team member (Team owner only)

Navigate to **Teams** and click on the **Edit** button.

<img src="/images/cloud/team-view.png" />

Navigate to the **Members** tab and click on the **Remove** button to remove a user.

<img src="/images/cloud/team-member.png" />
