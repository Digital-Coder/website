# Introduction

## What is Umami?

Umami is a simple, fast, privacy-focused, open-source analytics solution. It is a great alternative to Google Analytics
because it gives you total control of your data and does not violate the privacy of your users.

Check out the [Getting started](/docs/getting-started) section to get up and running in minutes!
