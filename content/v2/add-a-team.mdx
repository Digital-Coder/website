# Add a team

Log into Umami and click on **Settings** in the header.

<img src="/images/v2/header.png" />

Then navigate to **Teams** and click on the **Create team** button.

<img src="/images/v2/add-team.png" />

Fill out the form details and click the **Save** button.

<img src="/images/v2/add-team-form.png" />
